Lesson 2: Saving Strategies

Effective Saving Strategies

Focus on setting aside a portion of your income regularly.
Prioritize building an emergency fund, and consider automating savings to ensure consistency.
Look for high-yield savings accounts to maximize interest earnings.

Examples of Saving Strategies:

  • Pay Yourself First

    • Automatically save a portion of your income before spending it on other expenses.
    This strategy prioritizes your savings and ensures you build a financial cushion or achieve your savings goals.
    Set up automatic transfers to a savings account or investment fund to make this process easy and consistent.

  • Budgeting

    • Track your income and expenses to understand where your money goes and identify areas where you can cut back.
    Create a budget by categorizing your expenses into needs and wants, and set limits for each category.
    Regularly review and adjust your budget to stay on track and maximize your savings potential.

  • Emergency Fund

    • Save 3-6 months' worth of living expenses to cover unexpected costs such as medical emergencies, car repairs, or job loss.
    This fund acts as a financial safety net, providing peace of mind and helping you avoid going into debt when unforeseen expenses arise.
    Keep this fund in a separate, easily accessible savings account.
