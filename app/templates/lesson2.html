Lesson 2: Saving Strategies

Effective Saving Strategies

Focus on setting aside a portion of your income regularly.
Prioritize building an emergency fund, and consider automating savings to ensure consistency.
Look for high-yield savings accounts to maximize interest earnings.

Examples of Saving Strategies:

  • Pay Yourself First

    • Automatically save a portion of your income before spending.

  • Budgeting

    • Track expenses and cut unnecessary costs to boost savings.

  • Emergency Fund

    • Save 3-6 months of expenses for financial security.
